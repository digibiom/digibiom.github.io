Mike Reddy

Michael R. Reddy is a public health entomologist with a background in vector-borne disease epidemiology. His work contributes to a more detailed understanding of the eco-epidemiological determinants of malaria and other vector-borne disease transmission and the means to prevent such transmission in resource-poor settings. His specialities include malaria and arboviral disease ecology, mosquito vector population dynamics, and molecular genetic methodologies used in anti-vector intervention design, implementation, monitoring, and evaluation.
